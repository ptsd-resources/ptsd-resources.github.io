what's this?

This is a collection of recommended self-help resources, focused around but not strictly limited to trauma, and organized by Current Emotional Problem!

I am not professionally certified, trained, or licensed in any way regarding matters of trauma, therapy, or psychology. I'm just a layperson with a lot of emotional baggage, and my top coping mechanism is compulsively reading, annotating, and applying every resource that seems like it might help me deal with it.

Obviously, that approach isn't for everyone, so I hope these notes and summaries may help you narrow down on useful resources for your own situation and needs, and accelerate your healing journey so you don't have to schlep through as much self-help literature as I did.

I originally wrote this as a longform article, and made this website for quicker reference when the article got too long. If you have any feedback or would like to recommend more resources, you can write to me here.
